## Information

Data for the U-values of the Non-Residential Buildings is taken from: Behrooz Bagherian, Stefan Swiderek, Dr. Andreas Hartung, & Julian Bischof. (2021).
                                                                      Vergleichswerte für den Energieverbrauch von Nichtwohngebäuden: Anpassung eines
                                                                      vorliegenden Berechnungstools zur Ableitung von neuen Vergleichswerten für Energieverbrauchsausweise.
                                                                      Bundesinstitut für Bau-, Stadt- und Raumforschung (BBSR), Bundesministerium des Innern, für Bau und Heimat.
                                                                      BBSR-Online-Publikation 37/2021.

For the retrofitting of buildings, there are three options based on the extent of improvements:
    Not retrofitted: The building remains in its original condition; no part of the building has been upgraded for energy efficiency.
    Partially retrofitted: Includes renovations to the basement ceiling, windows, and roof only.
    Fully retrofitted: Includes renovations to all parts of the building, such as the basement ceiling, windows, roof, and walls.

The surface estimation factors have been calculated. The Data for the calculation is taken from: [German non-residential building typology](https://github.com/IWUGERMANY/Nichtwohngebaeude-Typologie-Deutschland)
As there is no data on placement of windows, they are equally distributed.

ow1  = aw_fl [m2] / nrf_2 [m2]
win1 = fen_fl [m²] / nrf_2 [m2]
rwt1 = D_fl_wueoA_opak [m2] / nrf_2 [m2]
gf1 = UG_fl_wueuA / nrf_2 [m2]
